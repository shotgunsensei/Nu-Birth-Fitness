Questions and scoring:

Q1. When you try to restart your health/fitness routine, what usually happens?
A. I go hard for a few days, then crash. → all_or_nothing
B. I restart the same plan over and over. → stuck_loop
C. Life gets too chaotic and I fall off. → overwhelmed
D. I struggle to even recognize what I need anymore. → lost_herself

Q2. Which phrase feels most true right now?
A. “If I can’t do it perfectly, I feel like I failed.” → all_or_nothing
B. “I know what to do, but I keep ending up back here.” → stuck_loop
C. “Everyone needs something from me all the time.” → overwhelmed
D. “I don’t feel like myself anymore.” → lost_herself

Q3. What is your biggest obstacle?
A. Consistency after motivation fades → all_or_nothing
B. Breaking old habits and cycles → stuck_loop
C. Time, stress, kids, family, work → overwhelmed
D. Energy, confidence, identity, self-worth → lost_herself

Q4. How do you feel when you miss a workout or meal plan?
A. Like I ruined everything → all_or_nothing
B. Like I’m back at square one again → stuck_loop
C. Like it was impossible anyway → overwhelmed
D. Like I’m disappointed in who I’ve become → lost_herself

Q5. What kind of plan would help you most?
A. A flexible plan that doesn’t punish imperfection → all_or_nothing
B. A pattern-breaking reset with accountability → stuck_loop
C. A simple routine that works inside real mom life → overwhelmed
D. A confidence rebuild that reconnects me to myself → lost_herself

Q6. What best describes your energy?
A. Comes in bursts, then crashes → all_or_nothing
B. Feels stuck in the same cycle → stuck_loop
C. Drained by responsibilities → overwhelmed
D. Low because I feel disconnected from myself → lost_herself

Q7. What do you want most in the next 90 days?
A. Stop quitting when it isn’t perfect → all_or_nothing
B. Finally stop starting over → stuck_loop
C. Lose weight without adding more chaos → overwhelmed
D. Feel confident, attractive, and alive again → lost_herself

Q8. How ready are you for support?
A. I need structure that keeps me from going extreme → all_or_nothing
B. I need accountability to break the loop → stuck_loop
C. I need someone to simplify this for my life → overwhelmed
D. I need someone to help me find myself again → lost_herself

Q9. What happens if nothing changes over the next 6 months?
A. I’ll keep repeating the same intense restart/crash cycle → all_or_nothing
B. I’ll still be stuck in the same loop → stuck_loop
C. I’ll keep putting myself last → overwhelmed
D. I’ll feel even further away from who I used to be → lost_herself

Scoring:

Highest score wins.
Tie breaker:
Use Q9 first, then Q8, then Q7.
Save score breakdown and answers.
